Ananya Bajaj

Data Scientist | Ex-AI/ML @NASA

I hold a Master’s degree in Data Science from the University of Washington, Seattle, with research focus on Natural Language Processing. I had the honor of serving as the graduate commencement speaker for the Class of 2024. In the summer of '24, I interned at the NASA Ames Research Center as an AI/ML Intern, contributing to innovative projects within the NASA Aeronautics Research Institute. I have 2+ years of professional and research experience, spanning diverse industries—including aerospace, aviation, healthcare, and retail, where I have tackled complex, cross-domain data challenges.

Beyond coding and algorithms, I enjoy writing, whether it’s telling stories or talking tech. In the evenings, you’ll usually find me dancing Salsa, Bachata, or Bollywood!
